# Translation Guidelines

- Target language: Japanese (標準的で丁寧な書き言葉).
- Preserve key proper nouns such as 星雲大師 or 佛光山 in Chinese characters; add explanatory Japanese terms only when needed for clarity.
- Maintain the original paragraph segmentation and numbering, pairing each Chinese paragraph with its Japanese rendering.
- When paragraphs contain quoted mottos or slogans, render them in Japanese quotation marks while keeping original Chinese key terms if they are established phrases.
